Get the StadiaStudios App

Add our website to your home screen for a fast, app-like experience. It's easy, free, and works on any device.

On Mobile

  • 1. Tap the browser's menu icon. (This is usually the three dots or the share icon ).
  • 2. Select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu.
  • 3. Confirm the name, then tap "Add". The app icon will now appear on your device's home screen.

On PC (Chrome & Edge)

  • 1. Look for the install icon in the address bar. (It looks like a computer with a download arrow ).
  • 2. Click the icon and follow the prompts to install the app.
  • 3. The StadiaStudios app will open in a new window and a shortcut will be added to your desktop and Start Menu.
