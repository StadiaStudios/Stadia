Add to Homescreen

If the automatic prompt didn't show up, you can manually add this app to your homescreen.

For iOS (iPhone & iPad)

  1. Tap the Share button . It's usually at the bottom of the screen in Safari.
  2. Scroll down and select **Add to Home Screen** .
  3. Tap **Add** in the top-right corner.

For Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the three vertical dots in the top-right corner of the browser.
  2. Select **Add to Home screen** from the menu.
  3. A pop-up will appear. You can edit the name before tapping **Add**.
